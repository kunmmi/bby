Here’s a step-by-step guide to buying $BABY on PancakeSwap:

Step 1: Set Up Your Wallet

Download a wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet if you don’t have one already. Add the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network to your wallet. For MetaMask: Go to Settings → Networks → Add Network and input the BSC details.

Step 2: Fund Your Wallet with BNB

Buy BNB (Binance Coin) from a trusted exchange (e.g., Binance). Send the BNB to your wallet address. Ensure you have a small amount of extra BNB for transaction fees.

Step 3: Go to PancakeSwap

Open PancakeSwap in your browser or use the dApp browser in Trust Wallet. Connect your wallet to PancakeSwap by clicking "Connect Wallet" in the top-right corner.

Step 4: Import $BABY Token

Copy the contract address, on PancakeSwap, click "Select a Token" in the "Swap" section. Paste the $BABY contract address and add the token.

Step 5: Adjust Settings

Set the slippage tolerance to account for transaction tax (check the project details for the exact rate; 7–8% is common). Click the gear ⚙️ icon to adjust slippage. Enter the amount of BNB you want to swap for $BABY.

Step 6: Confirm the Swap

Review the transaction details. Click "Swap" and confirm the transaction in your wallet.

Step 7: Add $BABY to Your Wallet

After the transaction is confirmed, manually add $BABY to your wallet using the contract address. In MetaMask or Trust Wallet, go to "Import Tokens" and paste the contract address. You’re now a proud holder of $BABY! 🚀
